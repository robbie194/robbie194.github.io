Built and studied optical imaging systems for computational reconstruction, including low-magnification large-field-of-view microscopy, Fourier ptychographic super-resolution, and fiber-based single-pixel imaging.

This research direction focuses on recovering high-quality visual information from constrained optical measurements by combining optical system design, inverse reconstruction, and learning-based image enhancement.
