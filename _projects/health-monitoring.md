Developed algorithms for contactless monitoring of driver and passenger health status from cockpit camera video. The pipeline combines face detection, facial keypoint detection, signal filtering, detrending, and frequency-domain analysis to estimate indicators such as heart rate, heart rate variability, blood oxygen saturation, and respiration rate.

This work connects real-time computer vision, physiological signal processing, and embedded deployment constraints in intelligent vehicle scenarios.
