Served as feature owner for object detection and tracking algorithms in camera preview systems. Responsibilities included algorithm design, model training and quantization, integration, effect verification, and resource optimization under mobile CPU and NPU constraints.

The work was commercially implemented on Huawei Mate50, P60, and Mate60 series phones, supporting long focal length moon mode, 4D focus, and related camera features.
